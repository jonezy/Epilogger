Here’s how to get started:

Step One: Make a new account
  • Create an account and update your profile picture, information, and add your Twitter and Facebook accounts.
Step Two: Have a look around
  • Try the Browse Events link at the top to see what’s happening around town.
  • You can browse by category at the bottom half of the page. You can see which events your Twitter friends are at!
  • Use the Feedback button at the left of your screen to give us your feedback, report a bug, or let us know if anything can be improved in the interface.
Step Three: Subscribe to events and check your dashboard
  • Find an event you like, click on it and subscribe to it with the subscribe button.
  • You should be able to see a feed of all your event subscriptions in your dashboard.
Step Four: Create an event
  • Create your own event and watch Epilogger track and record if for you by clicking “add an event” at the top of the screen.
